How Scoring Works

Fantasy Life uses a rotisserie (roto) system. Each player drafts one pick per category. At any point in the season, all 13 players are ranked against each other within each category based on their pick's performance. The best-performing pick earns 13 points; the worst earns 1 point.

Ties are handled with RANK.AVG — tied players split the available points evenly.

On top of baseline roto points, certain categories award bonus points for specific achievements — championships, Grand Slam wins, Oscar wins, and so on. Bonus points stack on top of roto points and can significantly swing the leaderboard.

Ranked by 2025 regular season win percentage. The NFL season is complete and frozen — standings will not change. Playoff performance earns bonus points.

NCAAF
College Football
Ranked by the final 2025 US LBM Coaches Poll position (released January 2026 after the CFP Championship). Lower poll rank = better. Championship bonus available.
NCAAB
College Basketball
Ranked by AP Poll position throughout the season. Lower poll rank = better. March Madness results earn bonus points.
Tennis
Tennis
Ranked by Official World Ranking (ATP/WTA). Men's and women's picks compete together; women's picks receive a +0.5 handicap on their world ranking to account for separate tours. Lower rank = better. Grand Slam results earn bonus points.
Amendment 7.20: women's major win bonus disputed but upheld.
Golf
Golf
Ranked by Official World Golf Ranking (OWGR), updated weekly. Lower rank = better. Major championship results earn bonus points.
NASCAR
NASCAR
Ranked by Cup Series championship points standings throughout the season. Higher points = better. Race wins and top finishes earn bonus points.
Actor
Actor
Ranked by a composite score based on award nominations and wins across major ceremonies. Oscar wins earn significant bonus points.
Actress
Actress
Ranked by a composite score based on award nominations and wins across major ceremonies. Oscar wins earn significant bonus points.
Musician
Musician
Ranked by Grammy performance. Wins and nominations accumulate through the awards cycle. Points are capped at 13 per Amendment 7.14.
Country
Country (Nation)
Each player picks a country (e.g. USA, France, Brazil). Ranked by performance in the major international event of the year — Olympic total medal count in Olympic years, or FIFA World Cup finish in World Cup years. Roto ranking among peers plus bonus points for top finishes.
Stock
Stock
Ranked by year-to-date percentage change of the picked stock or ETF. Updated throughout the year. Higher YTD gain = better.
